I asked someone for a quote to print the parts as per the main original file (minus the arms which are now more usually replaced by Traxxis arms)

I got this back from Alex of 3dprintanddesigner@gmail.com

"I looked over the parts.

You have multiple options. Depending on material density and resolution (0.2mm-0.4mm layers) it can cost from 54 to 85 pounds for the entire set. 54 pounds would be a 5% structural fill with 0.4mm layers, 85 pounds would be for 95% fill and 2mm layers. Or we can go somewhere in between depending on how much structural rigidity you want.

95% fills with 0.2mm layers will not to break after one year of brutal handeling.

I can give you better deals depending on how many you want. More are cheaper.
We can haggle a bit.

Regards,
Alex"

I am sure he would be happy to hear from anyone else in the UK / Rest of the EU about printing and supplying the parts.

A 5% infil might require additional strengthening which I am sure could be done with some product or other.
