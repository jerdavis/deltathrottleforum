Re: Suitable Springs (and other parts) from Amazon Supply?

by SC-Maik » Thu Apr 09, 2015 7:17 am

You can trim legs to a shorter length.

Thing is that wire's OD is slightly larger. You will have to modify 3D design of parts that hold springs in place, so you eliminate the chance of breaking small printed parts when you tighten screws.

If you don't have the knowledge (or 3d software) to do so, I can always do it for you (and everyone else that doesn't have access to McMaster's springs).

Just give me a shout.
