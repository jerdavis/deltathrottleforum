I'm looking to get parts to Canada. I've found sources for most of it and decided to print the arms to cut down on finding McMaster alternatives. I'll post my alternative sources for Canada in this post once I've exhausted my self searching for the last parts.

Suppliers by part for Canada (Edit):

Hat Switch: Digikey

10 Wire Ribbon Cable: Sparkfun or Canada Robotix

Hollow Ball Rod Ends: amazon.ca

USB Cable: NCIX but really anywhere will work I just like them
