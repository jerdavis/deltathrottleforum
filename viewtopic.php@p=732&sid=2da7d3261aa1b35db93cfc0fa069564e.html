I'm looking to get parts to Canada. I've found sources for most of it and decided to print the arms to cut down on finding McMaster alternatives. I'll post my alternative sources for Canada in this post once I've exhausted my self searching for the last parts.

Suppliers by part for Canada (Edit):

Hat Switch: Digikey
Momentary microswitch: Digikey
Potentiometers: Digikey

SparkFun 3.3V ProMicro: SparkFun or Canada Robotix
10 Wire Ribbon Cable: Sparkfun or Canada Robotix

Hollow Ball Rod Ends: amazon.ca

USB Cable: NCIX but really anywhere will work I just like them

People bought parts from Amazon supply earlier, I remember.
As for myself, I got parts from various places.

Springs: http://www.industrial-springs.com/
Torision springs: T072-120-500L
Z home position spring: E05000372250S

Rod ends for the upper pivot arm: http://www.igus.de/
igubal® Gelenkkopf

Hall effect sensors & magnets: http://ams.com/eng (ordered as free samples)
Hall effect sensor: AS5600
Diametrically magnetized magnet: Any "magnet for rotary position sensor" will work
