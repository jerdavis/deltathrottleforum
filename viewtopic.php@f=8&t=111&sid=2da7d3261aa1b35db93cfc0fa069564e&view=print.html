by jeruw
My wiring is kind of a mess right now too. I'm fixing it in my design by creating a second base where the arduino will be mounted and the wiring run. You can see that in my photos here: https://www.flickr.com/photos/24411428@ ... 953168623/ or in my build thread. SC-Maik says he's going to do something similar with his 3d printed throttle, you can check that out in his build thread. He also has some nice looking wiring in his thread going towards a separate breakout board mounted in the middle of his base.

Another option you might try if you have your own 3d printer would be to download and print this enclosure http://www.thingiverse.com/thing:320421 It looked like it would fit when I placed the 3d models together in Netfab, but that was without springs in place. If it does fit you could bolt that down to house your arduino. To do the cable hookups you could either solder on some header pins and use pin connectors to do the hookup, or you could keep your wires soldered to the board and route them out to each pot like you see in SC-Maik's build thread. I like his method of measurement, routing the cables through the solder points on each Pot, it looks clean. You could mark the cable at that point and cut and strip it for soldering.

Other than the wiring your build looks great! Love the color too. Good luck!
