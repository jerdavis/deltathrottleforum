Greetings from Greece,

Today I've ordered all 3D parts (excluding rods) for around 65USD. All black PLA 70%-100% filling, 100-200 microns resolution depending on part. Estimated printing time 21 hours.
Electronics (Digikey/Sparkfun) and Traxxas rod ends are on the way (order placed yesterday). I don't remember exact cost + shipping atm but I will be back with it.
Now I will have to find parts similar to McMaster from a European distributor.

I am so excited!
